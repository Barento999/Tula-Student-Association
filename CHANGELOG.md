## Known Issues

### Version 1.0.0

- File download is simulated (no actual files)
- Authentication is mock (no real backend)
- Data stored in LocalStorage (not persistent across devices)
- No email functionality (forms show success messages only)
- Images are placeholders (no real photos)

**Note**: These are intentional limitations of the frontend-only version and will be addressed in future backend integration.
